### About

Product designer by trade, design engineer in the making. By day I craft design systems and AI-assisted design-to-code workflows, build products for the African market, and teach myself to ship the interfaces I imagine one line of code at a time.

What I care about most is the seam where **design meets engineering**, and giving the craft away through community.
